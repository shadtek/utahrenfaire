Directions

Directions to Electric Park from the North: Take I-15 south. Take exit 284 (Alpine/Highland). Turn right and proceed west on Clubhouse Drive to the light at Ashton Blvd. Follow Ashton Blvd south and Electric Park will be on your left.


Directions to Electric Park from the South: Take I-15 north. Take exit 282 (2100 N) Turn Left onto 1200 W. Turn right onto Thanksgiving Way. Turn left onto Ashton Blvd. Follow Ashton Blvd and Electric Park will be on your right.
