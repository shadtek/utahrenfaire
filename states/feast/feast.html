These tickets do not include general admission to the faire.

1. Please check in at 5:45pm. If you would like to enter the faire sooner, general admission will be an extra charge.

2. The last joust of the evening is at 6:00 pm and you will get preferred and covered seats. If you are in costume you may be invited to sit with royalty!

3. 7:00 pm feast with entertainment.

4. No children under age 8 please.

5. Seating will be at long tables and we will try to put groups together. If you require group seating please come to the tent immediately after the joust.

The King's Feast is held in a large tent and it includes a multi-course meal with entertainment. Please choose the main course you want.
