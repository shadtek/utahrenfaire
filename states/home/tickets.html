Tickets

All standard tickets get you into the gates of the faire anytime from 10am to 8pm, on both days! You get access to all the entertainment, fun, education, and vendors that we bring each year.

This includes:

  • Daily jousting competitions
  • Armored Combat League fights
  • Magic shows
  • The Masters of Dueling
  • Birds of prey
  • Horse breed presentation
  • Live renaissance and medieval music performances all over the park from Stary Olsa, The Harp Twins, and many others
  • The artisan village full of historical education
  • The Society for Creative Anachronism demonstration area
  • The House of Tudor camp
  • Queen Elizabeth's music tent
  • The Vikings of Utah camp
  • Renaissance themed vendors of all types
  • Food vendors
  • ...AND SO MUCH MORE!

Queen Elizabeth's Joust and Feast tickets are for an enhanced experience that is above and beyond the standard ticket. Scroll down to see information and get more details.

Guests who are in costume and purchase general admission tickets at the gate receive $2 off a single-admission ticket!
Costume discount does not combine with the flyer you may have received.
We offer a military discount for veterans and active duty. To get the discount, show your ID at the gate on the day of the event. The service member must be present and show their ID at the time of purchase.

Veterans
50% off for the service member with the ID.
Tickets that qualify for this discount:
Adult, Student, or Senior


Active Duty 50% off for the service member, spouse, and dependents.
Tickets that qualify for this discount:
Adult, Student, or Senior, and / or Family Pass


The military discount is not available online as there is no way to submit an ID there yet. We apologize that we cannot apply the military discount to the educational excursions or the Queen Elizabeth's Joust and Feast.

Queen Elizabeth's Joust and Feast

$38 per guest.

A unique experience at the Utah Renaissance Faire. Covers the following: 5:30pm check-in, 6pm covered joust seats, 7pm feast with entertainment until 9pm.

The feast service will be family style and served in 5 courses to each table.

1st course: Assorted breads and butters
Gluten free available by request, ask your server.

2nd course: Potato leek soup
3rd course: Crisp green salad with vinaigrette dressing
4th course: Your choice of suckling pig or a giant turkey leg with a side of roasted corn on the cob
5th course: Peach cobbler
Gluten free available by request, ask your server.
