Queen Elizabeth's Joust and Feast

$38 per guest.

A unique experience at the Utah Renaissance Faire. Covers the following: 5:30pm check-in, 6pm covered joust seats, 7pm feast with entertainment until 9pm.

The feast service will be family style and served in 5 courses to each table.

1st course: Assorted breads and butters
Gluten free available by request, ask your server.

2nd course: Potato leek soup
3rd course: Crisp green salad with various dressings
4th course: Your choice of roast pork or a 2 pound turkey leg with a side of roasted corn on the cob
5th course: Peach cobbler
Gluten free available by request, ask your server.

  • Check-in is at 5:30pm for feast guests, if you want to check-in earlier please buy a general admission ticket.
  • If you are a feast ticket holder and wish to come to the renfaire before 5:30pm you will need to purchase a general admission ticket.
